# Prompt Gemini — Image borne kiosk Kelkun x Leclerc

## Prompt principal (pour Gemini Imagen / Gemini 2.0)

```
A modern interactive digital kiosk standing in a French Leclerc hypermarket DIY department (rayon bricolage). The kiosk has a clean, tall vertical touchscreen (about 130cm height) mounted on a sleek matte dark gray stand with rounded edges.

On screen: a teal (#00BFA6) and white interface showing "Besoin d'un artisan ?" as the main heading, with 6 category cards in a grid (plumbing, kitchen, electrical, tiling, AC, other) — each with a simple line icon. Below: a teal price estimate card showing "480 — 650 €" and a QR code. The UI uses the DM Sans font, white cards with subtle shadows on a light gray background.

The kiosk has a small "kelkun × E.Leclerc" co-branding badge at the top. The physical kiosk frame is matte dark anthracite with subtle rounded corners, similar to a modern self-checkout terminal.

Environment: bright hypermarket aisle with products on shelves in the background (blurred), white industrial ceiling, polished concrete floor. A customer (30s, casual, French) is touching the screen with one hand.

Style: photorealistic product visualization, retail environment photography, clean commercial render. Bright even lighting, slight depth of field on background. No text on the image except what's on the screen.
```

## Prompt alternatif (plus court, pour Imagen 3)

```
Photorealistic render of a sleek vertical touchscreen kiosk in a French hypermarket DIY aisle. The screen shows a teal and white service booking interface with "Besoin d'un artisan?" heading, category icon grid, price estimate, and QR code. Matte anthracite stand, co-branded "kelkun × E.Leclerc". A customer is interacting with the touchscreen. Bright retail environment, blurred product shelves in background. Commercial product photography style.
```

## Negative prompt (si Gemini le supporte)

```
No cartoon, no illustration, no flat design, no cluttered background, no neon lighting, no text watermarks, no stock photo feel
```

## Parametres recommandes

- Format : 16:9 ou 4:3 paysage (pour slide de presentation)
- Ou format : 9:16 portrait (pour montrer la borne en pied)
- Style : Photorealistic
- Qualite : High
